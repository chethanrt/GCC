Engagement Models

Your GCC, your way

Where are you in your GCC journey? 

Three structured engagement models, each designed around a different stage, from building from scratch to optimizing a center that isn't delivering its potential.

Build · Operate · Transfer

Starting from zero

We build, operate, and optimize your GCC before transferring full ownership. We handle everything including entity setup, location selection, leadership hiring, technology infrastructure, compliance, and operations, ensuring smooth transitions and complete autonomy once established.

Right for you if: you're setting up your first India GCC and want a partner who reduces risk from day one, not just cost. 

Managed GCCs

Already running, want better 

We manage your GCC's infrastructure, operations, and advanced technology needs, allowing you to focus on core business objectives. Our support ensures your center remains agile, scalable, and aligned with long-term goals, without the overhead of running the operational layer yourself.

Right for you if: your GCC exists but isn't delivering to its potential, or you need an experienced operator to take the wheel.

Managed Centers of Excellence (COE)

Deep expertise, on demand 

Our COEs focus on Cloud, Data & AI, Cybersecurity, and QA, driving innovation and operational efficiency. These hubs deliver expert talent and proven technology, ensuring continuous improvement across key business areas without standing up a full centre. 

Right for you if: you need a specialized, high-performing team fast, integrated directly with your engineering org.
